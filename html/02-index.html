WebSwitch Control

WebSwitch is an innovative project aimed at simplifying the way we interact with our devices and appliances. By creating a user-friendly website, we enable seamless remote control of various items at the click of a button.

01

Rapid Control

Empower your screen with RapidControl for Mac. The button, positioned right next to your Mac's image, brings your screen to life with a simple press, putting you in command without delay.

02

LuminaShift

Enter LuminaShift. Picture a button beside your bulb image. With a simple press, experience effortless bulb change, transforming your illumination instantly.

03

ChromaTrio

Enter ChromaTrio: envision three buttons alongside blue, yellow, and red color boxes. Press for instant color transformation. Effortlessly switch between hues.
